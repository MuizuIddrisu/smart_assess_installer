What is Smart Assess?

Smart Assess is a desktop school management system designed specifically for Ghanaian basic schools — from KG to JHS. It runs directly on your school's Windows computer with or without internet. When you reconnect, it automatically syncs all your data to the cloud so no work is ever lost.

Unlike web-based systems that break the moment your internet goes down, Smart Assess keeps working. Your teachers enter scores, attendance, and fees in the classroom — and everything is ready for report card printing day without a single data entry scramble.
